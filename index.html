🌟 Who We Are

We Turn Hospital Rooms into Birthday Parties

Kids in the hospital miss out on so much — especially birthdays. We think that's something we can fix. The Confetti Project works with hospitals and child life specialists to bring full, themed birthday celebrations right to a child's bedside. Cake, balloons, gifts, the works.

We're a Georgia-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit focused on children ages 1–18 in extended inpatient care. Every celebration is completely free for the family. We're just getting started, and we need your help to bring the party to as many kids as possible.

"During my time in the hospital as a child, my mother made every celebration meaningful — creating moments of normalcy and honoring the gift of life."

— Lynn Lilly, Co-Founder & Childhood Cancer Survivor
🎈 What We Do

Birthday Parties, Delivered to the Hospital

We handle everything — the family just tells us what their child loves, and we show up with a full celebration.

🎉

Themed Birthday Parties

Superheroes, unicorns, dinosaurs — whatever the kid is into, we build the party around it. Decorations, plates, banners, all matching.

🏥

Hospital Partnerships

We work directly with hospital staff and child life specialists to make sure every celebration is safe, coordinated, and joyful.

📦

Celebration Kits

Each party includes balloons, banners, a birthday cake, wrapped gifts, a crown, craft activities, and of course — confetti.

Picture This...

A hospital door opens. Balloons bounce off the ceiling. A banner with the child's name goes up. Their favorite character on a birthday plate. A real cake with real candles. Wrapped gifts picked just for them. A crown — because today, they're royalty.

For one moment, the hospital disappears. It's just a kid having a birthday party. That's what your donation makes possible.

The Story Behind The Confetti Project

🎨
Lynn Lilly
Co-Founder

Lynn is a childhood cancer survivor who spent many of her own birthdays in the hospital. Her mother's dedication to making every celebration meaningful planted the seed for The Confetti Project. ❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Where do donations go?
+
Directly to birthday celebrations — cake, balloons, decorations, gifts, and supplies. We're a lean operation and we stretch every dollar as far as it can go.
Are donations tax-deductible?
+
Yes! We're a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
How can I volunteer?
+
Just email us at hello@theconfettiproject.co or use the form on this page! We need help with party setup, kit assembly, deliveries, card writing, community outreach, and event planning. No experience needed.
What hospitals do you work with?
+
We're based in Georgia and building partnerships with hospitals across metro Atlanta and beyond. If your hospital wants to partner, reach out!
Can my company sponsor the Gala?
+
Absolutely! We have sponsorship tiers starting at $500. Email us at hello@theconfettiproject.co and we'll send you the details. Your brand gets visibility at the event and across our platforms.
How do I join the board?
+
We're actively building our board and looking for people with passion and expertise in areas like fundraising, healthcare, legal, finance, or marketing. Drop us an email — we'd love to chat.
